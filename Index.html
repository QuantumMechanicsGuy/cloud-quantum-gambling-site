What is Cloud Quantum Gambling?

Originally, "quantum gambling" referred to a cryptographic protocol in which two players interacted directly via a quantum channel, using quantum mechanics to ensure fairness and unpredictability.

In contrast, cloud quantum gambling repurposes the power of quantum computers in a modern, cloud-based environment.
